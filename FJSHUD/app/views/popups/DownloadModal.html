HUD Softphone Quick Start Guide

What is it? HUD Softphone allows you to make and receive calls with your computer.

Is it necessary? No. If you solely use a desk phone to make and receive calls, that is all you need. However, there are a few important reasons why you may want to download HUD Softphone:

  • You use or want to use softphone functionality with HUD (most commonly with a computer headset)
  • You do not have a desk phone
  • You use Fonality call controls inside your Salesforce application

If you decline, you can download from Preferences at a later date.
